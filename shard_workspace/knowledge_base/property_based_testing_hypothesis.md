## Key Concepts
- **Property-Based Testing**: A type of software testing that verifies properties or invariants about a program's behavior rather than specific outcomes.
- **Testing**: The process of evaluating a system to determine whether it meets the requirements and behaves as expected.
- **Python**: An interpreted, high-level programming language known for its readability and simplicity.

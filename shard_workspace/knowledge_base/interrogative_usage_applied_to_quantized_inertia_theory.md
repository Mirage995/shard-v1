## Key Concepts
- **Quantized Inertia (QI)**: Theory proposing inertia modification at low accelerations via Hubble-scale Casimir effect constraining Unruh radiation
- **Galaxy Rotation Curve Problem**: Stars at galaxy edges rotate faster than Newtonian predictions allow, motivating dark matter or modified physics
- **Dark Matter Hypothesis**: Prevailing explanation invoking invisible matter halos, yet undetected despite extensive experimental searches
- **Modified Newtonian Dynamics (MoND)**: Alternative modifying Newton's laws below a₀ ≈ 10⁻¹⁰ m/s², with arbitrary parameter but consistent mathematics
- **Hubble-scale Casimir Effect**: Cosmic horizon constrains quantum vacuum wavelengths, creating asymmetric pressure modifying inertia
- **Derivation Flaws in QI**: Renda (2019) identified fundamental mathematical errors in McCulloch's discrete blackbody radiation spectrum formulation
- **Pioneer Anomaly**: Unexplained spacecraft deceleration QI claims to explain through modified inertia effects
- **Unruh Radiation**: Thermal particles perceived by accelerating observers; proposed as inertia source when horizon-constrained in QI

## Key Concepts
* Transfer Learning: a technique used in machine learning for domain adaptation with applications in computer vision and natural language processing
* Catastrophic Forgetting: a crucial issue in continual learning that hinders the ability of models to learn from new tasks without forgetting previously learned knowledge
* Protein Folding: a complex problem in bioinformatics and structural biology that involves predicting the 3D structure of proteins
* Domain Adaptation: a technique used to adapt a model trained on one domain to another domain with a different distribution
* Continual Learning: a paradigm in machine learning that involves learning from a stream of data and adapting to new tasks and environments

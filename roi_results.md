## Architecture Components Active in SHARD Mode

- **Episodic memory**: past session history injected into Attempt 1 prompt
- **Knowledge bridge**: GraphRAG context from NightRunner study sessions
- **Concurrency simulator**: auto-detects threading tasks, probes race conditions before LLM call
- **Swarm engine**: Architect + Coder + parallel specialized reviewers (Concurrency, Security, EdgeCases, Performance, DataIntegrity)
- **Feedback loop**: up to 5 attempts with pytest output parsed and fed back to LLM
